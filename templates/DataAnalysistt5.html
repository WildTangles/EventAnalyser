tt¯ decay - data samples

The full dataset is composed of several real experimental data samples and simulated theoretical data samples. The experimental data samples (DataEGamma and DataMuons) contain the collision data at a center-of-mass energy of 8 TeV collected by the ATLAS detectors in 2012. They each contain a compound signal generated from a variety of processes. The theoretical data samples (e.g. Diboson WW, tt¯ -> Jets, etc.) contain data from Monte Carlo simulations of Standard Model processes and a selection of Beyond the Standard Model signals. They each contain the expected signal for the decay process that they're named after.

Try re-running the analyses with only the tt¯ -> Jets and tt¯ -> I + X simulated data samples to get a clearer sense for the expected contribution of the tt¯ decay channel to the overall signal!
